Edinburgh is a city of distinct neighbourhoods, each with its own history and character. From the cobbled streets of the Old Town and the Georgian avenues of the New Town to the seaside promenade of Portobello and the leafy suburbs of Morningside and Marchmont, there is something for every taste. The city council also divides Edinburgh into four administrative localities (North East, North West, South West and South East) which collectively encompass these districts.
